Volunteer Walking Group Leaders Needed

We are looking for friendly volunteers to lead new walking groups for people aged 60 and over.

Walking groups help older people stay active, meet others and feel more connected to their local community.

What will you do?

As a volunteer walking group leader, you will:

  • welcome people to the walking group
  • lead a safe and friendly local walk
  • encourage group members to walk together
  • help new members feel comfortable
  • contact the program organiser when needed

Who are we looking for?

You may be suitable for this role if you:

  • enjoy walking and meeting new people
  • are friendly, patient and reliable
  • can communicate clearly with a small group
  • are interested in helping older people
  • can attend regularly

Do I need experience?

You do not need to be a fitness instructor. However, volunteers must feel comfortable leading a small group.
